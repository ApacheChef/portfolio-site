About me

With 18 years in IT helping end-users, managing and deploying servers and network equipment, vendor relations, Microsoft Dynamics GP, Microsoft SQL Server, and department management, I'm making the switch to full-stack development.

I've had a strong interest in software development since childhood and now find myself in a position to make it my career instead of just a hobby. I care about producing clean, readable code, and faithful implementations of design specs. I enjoy working as part of a team, solving problems, learning, and refining my solutions to make them the best they can be. Aside from development, I enjoy playing guitar, learning to play the drums, picking up skateboarding, reading on varied topics from cosmology to personal improvement, dabbling with game development, and spending time with friends and family.
